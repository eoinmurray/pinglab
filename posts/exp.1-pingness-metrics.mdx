In this post we will investigate and compare three metris of "PINGness".

1. Autocorrelation peak of the E population rate.
2. Mean pairwise E spike cross-correlation peak.
3. Lagged Coherence.

The lagged coherence metrics comes from this paper:

> Fransen, A. M. M., van Ede, F., & Maris, E. (2015). *Identifying neuronal
> oscillations using rhythmicity*. NeuroImage, 118, 256–267.
> https://doi.org/10.1016/j.neuroimage.2015.06.003

The main comparison will be metrics vs seed of mild input noise so very similar
rasters are generated and we will see how consistent the metrics are across
seeds.
