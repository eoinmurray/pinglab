Some notes:
- Increasing $g_{ei}$ (excitatory to inhibitory coupling) tends to decrease the
  excitatory firing rate due to increased inhibitory input.
- Some complex dynamics appear at intermediate $g_{ei}$
  values, leading to irregular firing patterns.
- Eyeballing it, rhythmicity would start high and decrease as $g_{ei}$ increases.
- we should make rhythmicity vs $g_{ei}$ plot to confirm this.
