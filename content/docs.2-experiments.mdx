### 1. Two-network transfer

Network A encodes an image, projects to B, and B decodes. Sweep coupling delay
to control relative phase. Compare AI/AI, PING/PING in-phase, PING/PING
out-of-phase, PING/AI. Enforce rate + energy matching. Fidelity vs phase curve
with peak at the excitable window, holding under rate matching.
