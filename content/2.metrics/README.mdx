## Mean rates

<ParameterTable
  path={`${path}/data/osc_metrics.yaml`}
  keys={['mean_rate_E', 'mean_rate_I']}
/>

- `mean_rate_E`: Mean excitatory firing rate (Hz).
- `mean_rate_I`: Mean inhibitory firing rate (Hz).

## Population ISI CV

<ParameterTable
  path={`${path}/data/osc_metrics.yaml`}
  keys={[
    'cv_pop_E', 
    'cv_pop_I',
    'cv_mean_E',
    'cv_mean_I'
  ]}
/>

- `cv_pop_E`: Coefficient of variation of inter-spike intervals (ISIs) for the excitatory population.
- `cv_pop_I`: Coefficient of variation of inter-spike intervals (ISIs) for the inhibitory population.
- `cv_mean_E`: Mean CV of ISIs across excitatory neurons.
- `cv_mean_I`: Mean CV of ISIs across inhibitory neurons.

## Pairwise Spike Count Correlation

<ParameterTable
  path={`${path}/data/osc_metrics.yaml`}
  keys={[
    'corr_EE_mean', 
    'corr_II_mean',
    'corr_EI_mean'
  ]}
/>

- `corr_EE_mean`: Mean pairwise spike count correlation between excitatory neurons.
- `corr_II_mean`: Mean pairwise spike count correlation between inhibitory neurons.
- `corr_EI_mean`: Mean pairwise spike count correlation between excitatory and inhibitory neurons

## EI Lag Statistics

<ParameterTable
  path={`${path}/data/osc_metrics.yaml`}
  keys={[
    'lag_EI_mean_ms', 
    'lag_EI_std_ms'
  ]}
/>

- `lag_EI_mean_ms`: Mean lag (in ms) of inhibitory spikes relative to excitatory spikes.
- `lag_EI_std_ms`: Standard deviation of the lag (in ms) of inhibitory spikes relative to excitatory spikes.

## Conductance Stats

<ParameterTable
  path={`${path}/data/osc_metrics.yaml`}
  keys={[
    'g_e_mean', 
    'g_i_mean',
    'g_ei_ratio',
    'g_e_cv',
    'g_i_cv'
  ]}
/>

- `g_e_mean`: Mean excitatory conductance.
