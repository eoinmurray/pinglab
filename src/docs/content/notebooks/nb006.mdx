## Abstract

External drive is one knob ([nb003](/notebooks/nb003/)); internal E→I→E coupling strength is the other. With the stim-window overdrive pinned at {scan.fixed_overdrive}× (flat input through the trial), this notebook sweeps the E→I coupling strength — walking the network from the async baseline (E and I effectively decoupled) through the emergence of gamma as the feedback loop closes.

## Method

A scan over the [oscilloscope *video*](/articles/ar011/) subcommand with stim-overdrive held at {scan.fixed_overdrive}× and **E→I strength** sweeping {scan.min} → {scan.max} over {scan.frames} frames; input rate {scan.input_rate_hz} Hz, $W_\text{in}$ init ({scan.w_in_mean}, {scan.w_in_std}). All knobs are hardcoded literals in *src/notebooks/nb006.py* per the runner contract.
