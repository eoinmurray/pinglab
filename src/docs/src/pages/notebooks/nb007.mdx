## Introduction

Single-model companion to [nb003](/notebooks/nb003/). Trains only the [*cuba*](/models/#cuba) model — current-based synapses, the first rung on the CUBA → PING ladder that adds the $(1-\beta)/dt$ drive compensation — at *dt* = {cfg.dt ?? "?"} ms on MNIST and publishes its own curves, firing-rate trace, and training video. No Δt sweep.

## Method

*cuba* with 1024 hidden units, Kaiming init, learning rate $\eta$ = {cell.lr ?? "?"}, and the one-shot $(1-\beta)/dt$ init-scale compensation applied at train-*dt* so the initial per-step drive matches *standard-snn* at this *dt*. Trained at *dt* = {cfg.dt ?? "?"} ms for *T* = {cfg.t_ms ?? "?"} ms per trial, on {cfg.max_samples ?? "?"} MNIST samples × {cfg.epochs ?? "?"} epochs. All hyperparameters are literals in *src/pinglab/notebooks/nb007.py*.
