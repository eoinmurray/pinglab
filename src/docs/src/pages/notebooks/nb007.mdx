## Introduction

Same recipe as [nb006](/notebooks/nb006/) (snntorch-style direct-LIF) but with an exponential synapse on the feedforward synapses *into the hidden layer* — i.e. the *standard-snn-exp* registry variant. Sits between *standard-snn* (no exp synapse) and *snntorch-library* (external reference) on the model ladder; a single-knob ablation that isolates the contribution of the synaptic kernel without changing readout, surrogate, or init.

## Method

Hyperparameters are a verbatim copy of [nb006](/notebooks/nb006/) per the [runner contract](/docs/the-notebooks/#runner-contract); only the model name differs (`standard-snn-exp` vs `standard-snn`). The new model registry entry instantiates *CUBANet* with `exponential_synapse=True`.

The three shared customisations are explained in [nb006](/notebooks/nb006/). The fourth — exponential synapse — is the difference being measured here:

- **exponential synapse** — incoming spikes drive a low-pass-filtered conductance trace into the hidden layer (decaying exponentially with τ_syn ≈ 2 ms by default) instead of an instantaneous current kick. Smooths the per-step drive into the hidden neurons, often reducing rate overshoot at the cost of slower drive build-up.
