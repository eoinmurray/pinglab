## Introduction

Single-model companion to [nb003](/notebooks/nb003/). Trains only the [*standard-snn*](/models/#standard-snn) — the snnTorch research-baseline LIF classifier — at *dt* = {cfg.dt ?? "?"} ms on MNIST and publishes its own curves, firing-rate trace, and training video. No Δt sweep; this is the rung-zero training profile in isolation.

## Method

*standard-snn* with 1024 hidden units, Kaiming init, learning rate $\eta$ = {cell.lr ?? "?"}, trained at *dt* = {cfg.dt ?? "?"} ms for *T* = {cfg.t_ms ?? "?"} ms per trial, on {cfg.max_samples ?? "?"} MNIST samples × {cfg.epochs ?? "?"} epochs. All hyperparameters are literals in *src/pinglab/notebooks/nb005.py*; the runner shells out to the shared *oscilloscope train* path.
