## Abstract

Gamma has a period of ≈15–30 ms, so a coarse integration step *dt* can't resolve the E→I→E round-trip. With the Poisson input overdrive pinned well past the PING threshold from [nb003](/notebooks/nb003/) (so the rhythm would be robust at fine *dt*), this notebook sweeps *dt* — isolating the timestep as the only knob that can break the oscillation.

## Method

A scan over the [oscilloscope *video*](/articles/ar011/) subcommand with stim-overdrive held at {scan.fixed_overdrive}× (firmly inside the stable-PING regime mapped by [nb003](/notebooks/nb003/)) while *dt* sweeps {scan.min} → {scan.max} ms over {scan.frames} frames. All knobs are hardcoded literals in *src/notebooks/nb005.py* per the runner contract.
