External drive is one knob ([nb002](/notebooks/nb002/)); internal E→I→E coupling strength is the other. With the stim-window overdrive pinned at {scan.fixed_overdrive}× (flat input through the trial), this notebook sweeps the E→I coupling strength from {scan.min} to {scan.max} — walking the network from the async baseline (E and I effectively decoupled) through the emergence of gamma as the feedback loop closes. Input rate and $W_\text{in}$ are bumped relative to nb002 / nb010 so E has enough baseline drive to recruit I at all without a stim pulse.
