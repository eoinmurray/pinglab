## Introduction

Stub entry for sequential MNIST (sMNIST). MNIST is presented one pixel (or one row) at a time over the trial window rather than as a full image, so the network has to integrate information across time to classify — a memory/dynamics test rather than an input-encoding test.

## Method

First-cell baseline to validate the pipeline: one [*cuba*](/models/#cuba) model with two hidden layers (64, 64), trained on sMNIST at *extra_small* tier (200 samples × 3 epochs). Each MNIST digit is presented one row at a time over 280 ms (28 rows × 10 ms/row) at *dt* = 1 ms. Depth ≥ 2 is required: single-hidden-layer networks bottleneck at the 28-input interface and fail to learn on sMNIST. This cell exists to prove end-to-end correctness of the dataset loader + row-by-row encoder + depth-2 ladder config, not to measure anything specific — the cell matrix comes after.
