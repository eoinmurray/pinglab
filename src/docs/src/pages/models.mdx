# Models

## SNNTorchLibraryNet

Wraps [*snn.Leaky*](https://snntorch.readthedocs.io/en/latest/snn.neurons_leaky.html) + fast-sigmoid surrogate.

$$
U_{t+1} = \beta\, U_t + W\, s_t + b \;-\; \theta\, S_t, \qquad S_t = \mathbf{1}[U_t \geq \theta] \tag{1}
$$

Reset by subtraction. $\beta = e^{-\Delta t/\tau_{\text{snn}}}$ with $\tau_{\text{snn}} = 10$ ms; $W$, $b$ unscaled by $\Delta t$.

## CUBANet

One class, three CLI variants selected by *discretisation* ∈ \{snntorch, zoh\} and *exponential_synapse* ∈ \{False, True\}.

### *discretisation="snntorch"* (standard-snn, standard-snn-exp)

$$
U_{t+1} = \beta\, U_t + W\, s_t + b, \qquad S_t = \sigma_{\text{surr}}(U_t - \theta) \tag{2}
$$

$$
U_{t+1} \leftarrow \begin{cases} 0 & \text{reset=zero, } S_t = 1 \\ U_{t+1} - \theta\, S_t & \text{reset=subtract} \end{cases} \tag{3}
$$

Bias-balloon derivation. Take expectation under mean input rate $r$: $\mathbb{E}[s_t] = r\Delta t$, so each step adds $Wr\Delta t + b$ on average. Fixed point:

$$
U_{ss} = \beta U_{ss} + Wr\Delta t + b \;\Longrightarrow\; U_{ss} = \frac{Wr\Delta t + b}{1-\beta} \tag{4}
$$

Using $1-\beta \approx \Delta t/\tau$ for $\Delta t \ll \tau$:

$$
U_{ss} \approx W r \tau \;+\; \frac{b\tau}{\Delta t} \tag{5}
$$

Spike contribution $W r \tau$ is $\Delta t$-invariant; bias contribution scales as $1/\Delta t$.

With *exponential_synapse=True* (standard-snn-exp), $\tau_{\text{syn}} = 2$ ms:

$$
g_{t+1} = e^{-\Delta t / \tau_{\text{syn}}}\,(g_t + W\, s_t), \qquad U_{t+1} = \beta\, U_t + (1-\beta)\, g_t + b \tag{6}
$$

### *discretisation="zoh"* (cuba)

Continuous-time LIF:

$$
\tau\, \frac{dV}{dt} = -V + I(t), \qquad I(t) = \sum_k W\, \delta(t - t_k) + b \tag{7}
$$

Exponential-Euler step (closed-form homogeneous decay) + zero-order hold on $I$ over $[t, t+\Delta t]$:

$$
V(t + \Delta t) = e^{-\Delta t/\tau}\, V(t) + \frac{1}{\tau} \int_0^{\Delta t} e^{-(\Delta t - u)/\tau}\, I(t + u)\, du \tag{8}
$$

ZOH on the spike train: replace within-step Diracs by a uniform pulse of height $W s_t / \Delta t$.

$$
V_{t+1} = \beta\, V_t + \frac{1}{\tau} \int_0^{\Delta t} e^{-(\Delta t - u)/\tau} \left[\frac{W\, s_t}{\Delta t} + b\right] du, \quad \beta = e^{-\Delta t/\tau} \tag{9}
$$

The integral evaluates to

$$
\frac{1}{\tau} \int_0^{\Delta t} e^{-(\Delta t - u)/\tau}\, du = 1 - \beta \tag{10}
$$

giving

$$
\boxed{U_{t+1} = \beta\, U_t + \frac{1 - \beta}{\Delta t}\, W\, s_t + (1 - \beta)\, b} \tag{11}
$$

Limits as $\Delta t \to 0$: per-spike kick $\to W/\tau$; per-ms bias $\to b/\tau$. Both $\Delta t$-invariant.

## COBANet

Conductance-based E + I populations (E:I = 4:1) with frozen recurrent matrices $W^{EE}, W^{EI}, W^{IE}$. With *ei_strength=0* the recurrent loop is off (feedforward COBA); otherwise PING.

Membrane:

$$
C_m\, \frac{dV}{dt} = -g_L(V - E_L) - g_e(V - E_e) - g_i(V - E_i) \tag{12}
$$

Collect on $V$:

$$
C_m\, \frac{dV}{dt} = -(g_L + g_e + g_i)\, V + (g_L E_L + g_e E_e + g_i E_i) \tag{13}
$$

Define $g_{\text{tot}} = g_L + g_e + g_i$, $\tau_{\text{eff}} = C_m/g_{\text{tot}}$, $V_\infty = (g_L E_L + g_e E_e + g_i E_i)/g_{\text{tot}}$:

$$
\tau_{\text{eff}}\, \frac{dV}{dt} = -(V - V_\infty) \tag{14}
$$

ZOH on $g_e, g_i$ → constant $\tau_{\text{eff}}, V_\infty$ over the step:

$$
V_{t+1} = V_\infty + (V_t - V_\infty)\,\exp(-\Delta t / \tau_{\text{eff}}) \tag{15}
$$

Synaptic conductances. With $\alpha = e^{-\Delta t/\tau_{\text{AMPA}}}$, $\gamma = e^{-\Delta t/\tau_{\text{GABA}}}$, $\tau_{\text{AMPA}} = 2$ ms, $\tau_{\text{GABA}} = 9$ ms:

$$
g^{E \to E}_{t+1} = \alpha\bigl(g^{E \to E}_t + W^{EE}\, s^{E}_t\bigr) + W_{\text{in}}\, s^{\text{inp}}_t \tag{16}
$$

$$
g^{E \to I}_{t+1} = \alpha\bigl(g^{E \to I}_t + W^{EI}\, s^{E}_t\bigr) \tag{17}
$$

$$
g^{I \to E}_{t+1} = \gamma\bigl(g^{I \to E}_t + W^{IE}\, s^{I}_t\bigr) \tag{18}
$$

Hard reset $V \leftarrow V_{\text{reset}}$ on threshold; refractory $\tau_{\text{ref}}^E = 3$ ms, $\tau_{\text{ref}}^I = 1.5$ ms. Dale's law: $W \geq 0$. Defaults: $W^{EE} = 0$, $W^{EI} \approx 1$ µS, $W^{IE} \approx 3$ µS. Recurrent matrices frozen at init; only $W_{\text{in}}, W_{\text{out}}$ train. $\Delta t$ ceiling at $\sim \tau_{\text{GABA}}$. Constants on [Parameters & Units](/parameters-and-units/#coba--ping-biophysical-constants).
