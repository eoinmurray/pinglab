\subsection{Summary of single neuron equations}

The model specialises in to two populations — E driven by excitation and inhibition, I by excitation only

\begin{equation}
C_m^E\,\frac{dV^E}{dt} = -g_L^E (V^E - E_L) - g_e^E (V^E - E_e) - g_i^E (V^E - E_i)
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
C_m^I\,\frac{dV^I}{dt} = -g_L^I (V^I - E_L) - g_e^I (V^I - E_e)
\end{equation}
