# Minimal Validation of PING Network

- Raster plot of A-OSC transition as $g_{ei}$ increases.
- Raster plot of A-OSC transition as $I_E$ increases.
- Firing rate vs input current for E and I populations.
- Power Spectral Density of E population firing rate.
- Cross-Correlogram between E and I populations.
- Population ISI Coefficient of Variation vs Synaptic Weight.
