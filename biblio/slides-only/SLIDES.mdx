## Cross-Correlogram

Temporal relationship between E and I populations.

- Peak at positive lag: I lags E by ~2-5 ms
- Confirms PING mechanism: E fires → recruits I → I inhibits E

## Population ISI CV

Coefficient of variation of inter-spike intervals.

- CV = 1: Poisson-like (irregular)
- CV < 1: Regular firing (oscillatory)
